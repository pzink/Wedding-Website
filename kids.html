Kids:

Your children are welcome to come to the lodge and join us for the informal events.

There are lots of fun lakeside activities for kids:
swimming,
boats and kayaks,
tennis courts,
and more!

If it's better or easier for you to bring your children,
then please do.

During the relatively short few hours of ceremony, reception, dinner and dancing, we will have babysitters and a "rec room" to keep them all occupied, and there will be special kid meals (chicken fingers, french fries, kale, etc.)
